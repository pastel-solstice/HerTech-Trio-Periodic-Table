🎯 Problem Statement

Over 60% of Class 9–12 students in Odisha study in schools without chemistry labs. They rely solely on static NCERT textbooks, with no interactive tools to visualise atomic structure, explore reactions, or test their knowledge. Existing platforms are either paywalled, English-only, or require high-speed internet.

💡 Our Solution

A zero-install, single-file chemistry platform that works offline after first load. It covers the full NCERT syllabus from Class 9–12, with an interactive periodic table, AI-powered tutoring (Claude), animated Bohr models, reaction predictor, challenge quizzes, and mnemonics in Odia and Hindi — specifically addressing the language barrier for Odisha students.
